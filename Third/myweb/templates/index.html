Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for the events?
You can register by visiting the registration page on this website. Simply fill out your Admission Number, Name, Course, Year, and select the event or team sport you'd like to join.
Is there a registration fee?
No, participation in the sports festival is completely free of charge for all students.
What sports will be available in the festival?
The festival will feature a variety of sports including basketball, cricket, badminton, athletics, as well as indoor games like chess, table tennis, and carrom. A detailed schedule of the events is available on the event page
Can spectators attend the events?
Yes, students are encouraged to come and support their friends. Spectators are welcome to cheer for their teams during the events.
Can I participate in more than one event?
Yes, students are allowed to participate in multiple events, as long as the event schedules do not overlap. Please check the schedule carefully before registering for multiple events.
